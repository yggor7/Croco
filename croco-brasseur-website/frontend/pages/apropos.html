Notre Histoire

A Bold Vision Born in Bujumbura

Croco Brasseur is the story of a passion for great beer, delicious food, and good times. Our establishment is designed to be a crossroads where friendliness, culinary art, and festive vibes come together.

Our name, Le Croco, honors the legendary crocodiles of Lake Tanganyika — a symbol of strength, heritage, and wild beauty. Inspired by both global trends and local roots, our team blends world-class flavors with the familiar rhythms of Bujumbura.
